About Restaurant


Tradition since 1975

"Our restaurant was founded in 1975 in Italy ,Rome and has been serving delicious dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients ever since. Our cozy and inviting space offers a mix of classic and contemporary dishes, including vegetarian and gluten-free options. Our friendly and attentive staff is dedicated to providing an excellent dining experience for all of our guests. We also have a full bar featuring an extensive selection of wines, beers, and cocktails. We are proud to have been a beloved part of the Rome community for years and look forward to welcoming you to our restaurant soon!"
