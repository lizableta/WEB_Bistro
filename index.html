About Restaurant


Tradition since 1975

"Our restaurant was founded in 1975 in Italy ,Rome located in the heart of downtown, this cozy restaurant offers a wide range of delicious dishes inspired by classic Italian cuisine. and has been serving delicious dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients ever since. From handmade pastas and wood-fired pizzas to succulent grilled meats and seafood, there is something for everyone on the menu. The restaurant boasts a warm and inviting atmosphere, with rustic wood décor and soft lighting creating a cozy and romantic ambiance. Whether you're in the mood for a romantic dinner for two or a casual meal with friends, this restaurant is the perfect choice. The attentive and friendly staff are always on hand to ensure a memorable dining experience. Be sure to save room for dessert – the tiramisu and gelato are not to be missed!

Our Menu


Appetizers:

  • Bruschetta (grilled bread topped with tomato, basil, and mozzarella)
  • Calamari fritti (deep-fried calamari)
  • Caprese salad (sliced tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil)

Pasta dishes:

  • Spaghetti carbonara (spaghetti with bacon, eggs, and Parmesan cheese)
  • Fettuccine alfredo (fettuccine with cream and Parmesan sauce)
  • Lasagna (layered pasta with meat and cheese)

Pizza

  • Margherita(tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil)
  • Pepperoni (spicy pepperoni sausage and mozzarella cheese)
  • Veggie (topped with a variety of vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and olives)
  • Hawaiian (topped with ham, pineapple, and mozzarella cheese)

Entrees:

  • Pollo alla Parmigiana (breaded chicken with tomato sauce and mozzarella)
  • Bistecca alla Griglia (grilled steak)
  • Salmon alla Griglia (grilled salmon)

Desserts:

  • Tiramisu (layered dessert with espresso, mascarpone, and ladyfingers)
  • Gelato (Italian ice cream)
  • Panna cotta (cooked cream dessert with fruit sauce)
